Upon the deck where salty breezes blow, El Capitan commands with eyes so keen, A master of the sea where currents flow, And of the kitchen, where his craft is seen.

With steady hand, he carves the ocean's prize, A lobster, fresh and bright from morning's catch, Each movement sure, beneath the azure skies, He works with grace, no challenge left to match.

In butter's golden pool, his dish takes flight, With garlic's fragrant kiss, a touch of lime, He conjures flavors, simple yet so bright, Transforming meals into a taste sublime.

El Capitan, of sea and stove the king, In every dish, his passion's song does sing.
