El Capitan, a traveler bold and free, Set sail for China’s distant, ancient lands, Where rivers flow and forests whisper, see, The temples rising where the green expands.

In markets bustling with life’s vibrant beat, He wandered, tasting all the land's delights, From fragrant teas to dishes rich and sweet, And lanterns glowing softly in the night.

Inspired by the flavors he did find, He learned the art of dumplings from a sage, With vegetables and spices well combined, He mastered recipes of every age.

El Capitan, with wisdom in his hand, Returned to share the tastes of China's land.
