Through Paris streets, El Capitan once roved, A culinary master of the arts. With chef’s resolve, his craft he deftly proved, Creating dishes that delight the hearts.

From markets fresh, he gathered champignons, And mustard seeds to blend with veal so fine. A secret sauce, a Parisian anon, Enhances flavors, rich like vintage wine.

Rognon à la moutarde, a bold delight, A triumph in the city’s bustling grace. El Capitan, beneath the city lights, Brings Paris charm to every warm embrace.

A master’s touch in kitchens far and near, A French cuisine, with soul and skill sincere.
