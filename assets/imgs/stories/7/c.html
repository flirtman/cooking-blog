Patate al Forno, golden, crisp, and bright, Begin the feast with fragrant herbs in tow. El Capitan, by midday’s warming light, Attends each slice as olive oils flow.

With garlic cloves and rosemary to blend, He roasts until the edges brown and curl. Each bite, a mix of earth and sea’s commend, The humble spud transformed, its flavors swirl.

Then comes the salmon, set to sizzle low, Its skin a crisp, its flesh a tender pink. Together, side by side, their warmth to show, A feast of taste and scent, in midday’s blink.

So Capitan delights in simple fare, His midday meal a craft beyond compare.
